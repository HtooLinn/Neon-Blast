HOW TO PLAY

📦 PLACING BLOCKS

Drag pieces from the bottom tray onto the 8×8 grid. A piece can only be placed if all its cells fit on empty squares. No rotation — plan strategically!

🔥 CLEARING LINES

Fill an entire row OR column to clear it and earn bonus points. Clearing multiple lines at once gives a big score multiplier!

💖 HEARTS (CLASSIC)

Some grid squares contain hearts. Place a block on a heart square to embed it. When that row or column clears, the heart is collected toward your level goal.

🎯 CLASSIC MODE

Each level has a heart target and a move limit. Collect the required hearts before running out of moves to advance. Each level gets harder!

♾️ ENDLESS MODE

Play forever and keep increasing your score. The game ends when no piece can be placed on the board. Try to beat your personal best!

🏆 SCORING

+1 per block placed. +10 × blocks per cleared line. +25 bonus per heart collected. Chain clears for multipliers!
